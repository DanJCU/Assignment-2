Goals

The website is to help the company expand thier business by promoting the upcoming feature as listed:

  • New pet store specializing on puppies
  • Veterinary service
  • Classes on setting up landscapes and koi ponds for residential and corporate customers.

While maintaining or improving the current business in fish products.

Success Evaluation

Maintainance of fish products sales as well as meeting of class registration quota.

All other advertising done other than the website will remain consistant for 3 months after its launch. Success is achieved when in those 3 months, if sales stay roughly the same or have an increase.

A quota for classes will be set with a student base of 15. We will open registering for future classes for customers for 3 months. Success is achieved when they are able to hold at least 2 classes per month at the end of the 3 months.

Target Audience

General public, with a focus towards pet owners, or those who would be interested in owning a pet, in the age group 15 to 25 years old.
