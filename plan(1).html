Goals

This website’s purpose is to sell existing fish products, as well as new expansions’ products and services of a pet shop specialising in puppies, veterinary and classes for landscaping and koi pond setup. This will be done by promoting the products and services to a youth client base, soliciting them to place orders and sign up for classes.

Success Evaluation

Maintainance of fish products sales as well as meeting of class registration quota.

All other advertising done other than the website will remain consistant for 3 months after its launch. Success is achieved when in those 3 months, sales remain constant or increase.

A minimum of 15 will be set for class registration quota, with registration for future classes open over the 3 months after site launch. Success is achieved when at the end of the 3 months, the class registration quota has been met for both landscaping and koi pond setup classes.

Target Audience

Teens and young adults, from age 15 to 25.

Though the site aims to cater to the general public, it will focus towards those who would be interested in owning pets or who are pet owners.

This will be done by using a ‘friendly’ and cosy looking design, with appealing images of fish/animals in the background.
