Landscaping classes

Landscaping is the process of furnishing features of an area of land or terrain, like surrounding trees, to make it more attractive.

In this class, learners will gain the fundamentals of landscaping, including design, gardening and elements (living, natural and abstract). Different settings and preferences are taken into account, with various tools that can be used to shape the environment to better improve pets' lifestyles.

Koi pond classes

Koi pond setup is a specialised area within landscaping which focuses on holding koi fish. These ponds help improve and maintain koi fishes' wellbeing and are also an attractive piece to have in your landscape.

This class provides learners with in-depth knowledge of setting up koi ponds. All you need to know about the specific environment for koi fish, such as aspects of construction, filtration, and lighting, can be learnt here. First time
koi fish owners will find this class especially useful.
